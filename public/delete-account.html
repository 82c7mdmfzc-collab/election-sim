Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Elector account and all associated data at any time.

Delete it yourself, in the app

The fastest way is right inside Elector:

  1. Open Elector and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Open the account panel (tap your Funds/coin pill on the main menu).
  3. Tap Delete account, then confirm Delete forever.

This immediately and permanently removes your account and its data — there is no recovery.

Or request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email us from the address linked to your account and we’ll delete your account and data within 30 days.

support@playelector.com

What gets deleted

Your account record and all associated data: email/login, permanent username, Campaign Funds, unlocked characters, lifetime stats, referral records, and any open lobby membership. Server security logs may be retained briefly as required for fraud/abuse prevention, then expire.
