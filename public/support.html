Frequently asked

How do I play?

Elector is a turn-based strategy game about winning the US Electoral College. Campaign across states, build coalitions, and race to 270 electoral votes — solo, against bots, or online with friends. A built-in tutorial walks you through your first game.

Do I need an account?

No. You can play anonymously and your progress is still saved to your device. If you'd like to keep your progress across devices, you can save it with a magic link sent to your email.

How does online multiplayer work?

One player hosts a lobby and shares the room code; others join with it. Turns are coordinated by our server so games stay in sync. If a game stalls, make sure all players have a stable connection and try rejoining with the same room code.

What are Campaign Funds?

Campaign Funds are the in-game currency you earn by finishing games. They're tracked on your account and can be spent in the in-game Shop. Replaying an already-finished game does not grant funds again.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email support@playelector.com from the address linked to your account (or include your account details) and we'll delete your data within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for details.
